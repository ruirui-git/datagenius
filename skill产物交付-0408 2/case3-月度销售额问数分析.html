月度销售额 & 订单量趋势

数据来源：dataclaw_donot_delete.olist_dws.dws_category_daily | 2017-01 ~ 2018-08

SELECT DATE_FORMAT(dt, 'yyyy-MM') as month,
    SUM(total_revenue) as monthly_revenue,
    SUM(order_count) as monthly_orders,
    SUM(total_revenue) / SUM(order_count) as aov
FROM dataclaw_donot_delete.olist_dws.dws_category_daily
GROUP BY DATE_FORMAT(dt, 'yyyy-MM') ORDER BY month
